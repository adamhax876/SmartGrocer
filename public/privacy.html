🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026 · We take your privacy seriously

Summary: SmartGrocer collects only the data necessary to run your store management system. We never sell your data to third parties. Your business data is yours, always.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Data Name, email, store name Account creation & authentication
Business Data Products, sales, inventory records Core platform functionality
Usage Data Page views, feature usage, timestamps Improve our platform
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device info Security & debugging

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SmartGrocer service
  • To send you important account notifications (verification codes, alerts)
  • To analyze and improve platform performance
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized use
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in secure MongoDB databases with the following protections:

  • Passwords: Hashed with bcrypt (12 rounds) — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with configurable expiration
  • Transmission: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Access control: Role-based access limiting who can see what

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Hosting/infrastructure providers under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal obligations: If required by law or valid legal process

5. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Rights)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal data at any time
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your business data in CSV/Excel format
  • Objection: Opt out of non-essential data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@smartgrocer.app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Business data (products, sales) is deleted immediately
  • Backup copies are purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregate usage statistics may be retained indefinitely

7. Cookies

SmartGrocer uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for authentication and session management
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use Google Analytics or advertising cookies

8. Children's Privacy

SmartGrocer is a business-to-business platform intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact & Data Controller

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
📧 privacy@smartgrocer.app
